The Best Movies According to Orlando Sama

My Top 3 Movies of All Time.

  1. Koe No Katachi

    2. A poignant story of a young man seeking redemption for his past bullying of a deaf classmate. It delves into themes of guilt, forgiveness, and finding one's voice.

  2. Avengers: Endgame

    3. The epic conclusion to the Infinity Saga, where the remaining Avengers embark on a time-bending mission to reverse Thanos's devastating snap and restore the universe.

  3. Life of Pi

    4. A visually stunning tale of survival and faith, following a young boy who finds himself adrift on a lifeboat with a Bengal Tiger after a shipwreck. It explores themes of resilience, hope, and the power of storytelling.
